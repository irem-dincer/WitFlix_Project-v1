★★★★☆ 7.3 IMDB 16+ 2hr 21min

The film tells the story of Joel Barish (Carrey) and Clementine Kruczynski (Winslet), an estranged couple who have each other's memories erased from their minds. The story follows Joel's journey through his memories as he watches his relationship with Clementine unravel. As the memories fade, Joel desperately tries to preserve his love for Clementine by hiding her in other memories.

Starring: Jim Carrey, Kate Winslet
